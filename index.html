  • Vancouver Aquarium

    • It contains thousands of incredible ocean species and amazing aquatic life animals. besides, it has the following attractions:
    1.An interactive 4D Experiences theater for watching the Underwater Wonders.
    2.A gift shop sells aquatic-themed gifts.
    3.A Wet Lab for children to touch the invertebrates animals.
    4.A Touch Pools can let students get hands-on with some of the mysterious and colorful animals living in British Columbia's tidepools. For instance, Giant sea cucumbers, Giant green anemones and Gentle pinky fingers.
    5.They have three On-Site Dining shops, namely Bicycle Bistro, Courtyard Cafe & Coffee Bar and Upstream Bar + Grill.

  • North Shore Tour Over Capilano Bridge

    • It is called Capilano suspension bridge park. It provides three attractions, that are On the Suspension Bridge, Treetops Adventure and Cliffwalk. The suspension bridge is 450 feet long and can take anywhere from 5 – 15 minutes to cross. It takes 2.5-3 hours walking through the park. The park offers free shuttle and free WiFi. The Canyon Lights shows run during the winter months of November to January.
